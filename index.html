Our Story

A Recipe Passed Down,
A Kitchen Built on Care

In Javanese tradition, Embah means grandparent - the elder whose kitchen was always warm, whose recipes were never written down, only remembered and shared. We started Embah to keep that feeling alive: real ingredients, honest techniques, and dishes that taste like they were made by someone who loves you.

Every order is cooked fresh in small batches - never mass-produced, never reheated from a freezer. Whether it's a lunchbox for one or a full spread for three hundred guests, it gets the same attention Embah would give it herself.
